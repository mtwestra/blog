### What is RSS?

RSS is an open standard for following blogs without an account or algorithm. Subscribe using a feed reader like [NetNewsWire](https://netnewswire.com/) (Mac/iOS, free), [Reeder](https://reeder.app/) (Mac/iOS), [NewsBlur](https://www.newsblur.com/) (web, Android, iOS) or [Feedly](https://feedly.com/) (web). New posts appear automatically in your reader — like email, but without the inbox.
